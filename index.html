Research Papers

Payments Under the Table and Optimal Income Taxation Job Market Paper [Link]

Abstract
I develop a model where firms and workers may underreport wages (PUT). A corporate tax alters the classic optimal income tax formula via a fiscal externality and by lowering the ETI when it discourages PUT. Using matched administrative and survey data from Peru, I show PUT is common and declines when the corporate tax rises.
